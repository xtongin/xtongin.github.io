Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Robotics and Automation, Nanjing University, Suzhou Campus. Previously, I served as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Multi-Scale Medical Robotics Center and as a Research Associate in the Department of Mechanical and Automation Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I received my B.S. from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2015, followed by a Ph.D. from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020.

Research Interests

  • Robotic planning and control

  • Hybrid feedback control

  • Multi-agent systems

  • Network formation
